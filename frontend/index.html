About Us

Born on a historic day—2nd April 2011, when India clinched its second World Cup with MS Dhoni’s iconic six—Spice and Smoke was founded with one simple dream: to bring people together over fire-grilled flavors and unforgettable meals. What started as a small passion project has grown into a vibrant space where friends, families, and food lovers gather to celebrate good times. At Spice and Smoke, we believe dining should be an experience. That’s why our buffet comes alive with an interactive grill system at every table, letting you craft your meal just the way you like it. From sizzling kebabs to perfectly seared veggies, every bite is a mix of flavor, fun, and a little bit of magic. Our philosophy is simple: fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a warm, welcoming vibe. Every dish is crafted with care by our expert chefs who pour their heart into the grill—because food is more than just fuel; it’s an experience to savor. Step into Spice and Smoke and let the aromas, the sizzling sounds, and the lively energy transport you. Whether you’re here to celebrate, relax, or simply indulge, we promise a meal that’s memorable, interactive, and deliciously yours. Come join us, grab the tongs, and be part of the flavor revolution. Your table—and the grill—is waiting.
